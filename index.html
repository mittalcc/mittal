About Us

We are a value-driven, customer-focused, creative and responsible fashion company. We are committed to always delivering fashion and quality at best price. We respect human rights in all our operations. We are built on solid foundations. Our business concept - fashion and quality at best price - is as clear as our values, which are based on fundamental respect for each individual and include a firm belief in our people. Our shared values are team work, simplicity, entrepreneur spirit, cost consciousness, straight forwardness and openness as well as striving for constant improvements. These values represent Mittal spirit and these are very same values as those on which Mittal was first founded by Mr Mukesh Mittal and Mr Pankaj Mittal in 1995 and the legacy is being carried by young & dynamic Mr Rohan Mittal at the helm.
